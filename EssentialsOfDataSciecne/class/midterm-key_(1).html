In [35]:
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

user_data.plot.scatter('FriendCount', 'TaggedArtistCount', s=0.2)
plt.plot(np.linspace(0,100,100), [linreg.intercept_ + linreg.coef_ * z for z in np.linspace(0,100,100)], c="red", marker=".", linestyle=":")
plt.show()

We see a very mild positive correlation between FriendCount (X) and TaggedArtistCount (Y). The correlation coeffecient is 0.048 and the best fit line has a Y-intercept=90.25 and slope m=0.618.

In the scatter plot, we see that there are many users that have few tagged artists and many friends, and conversely many users that have few friends and many tagged artists. Friend count is not a good predictor for tagged artist count, and vice versa.
